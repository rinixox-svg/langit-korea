리로 발음한다.
When the consonants ‘ㄱ, ㄷ, ㅂ, ㅅ, ㅈ’ are preceded by ‘ㄱ, ㄲ, ㅋ’, the preceding consonant is pronounced
as an unreleased [ㄱ] and the following consonant is pronounced as a tense sound.
