어간의 받침 ‘ㄴ, ㅁ’ 뒤에 결합되는 어미의 첫소리 ‘ㄱ, ㄷ, ㅅ, ㅈ’은 된소리로 발음한다.
When the final consonants ‘ㄴ’ and ‘ㅁ’ in verb stems are followed by the initial consonants ‘ㄱ, ㄷ, ㅅ,
ㅈ’ of endings, ‘ㄱ, ㄷ, ㅅ, ㅈ’ are pronounced as tense sounds.
