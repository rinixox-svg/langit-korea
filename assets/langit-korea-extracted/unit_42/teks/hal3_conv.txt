In compound words where an inserted ‘ㅅ’ is required for genitive function (when there is a pause),
the initial consonants ‘ㄱ, ㄷ, ㅂ, ㅅ, ㅈ’ of the following syllable are pronounced as a tense sound.
⑴ 빵-집[빵찝] 눈-동자[눈똥자] 손-가락[손까락] 산-새[산쌔]
