Insurance Related to Foreign Workers
고용허가제(Employment Permit System)로 입국한 외국인 근로자는 한국의 모든 근로자가 가입하는 4대 사회보험과 외국인 근
로자 전용 보험에 가입해야 합니다.
Foreign workers who enter Korea under the Employment Permit System must be enrolled in Korea’s Four Major Social
Insurances, as well as Insurance Exclusive to Foreign Workers.
