받침 ‘ㅍ’ 뒤에 ‘ㄱ, ㄷ, ㅂ, ㅅ, ㅈ’가 연결되는 경우, 앞 자음은 파열되지 않은 [ㅂ]으로 발음하고 뒤 자음은 된소리로 발
음한다.
When the final consonant ‘ㅍ’ is followed by ‘ㄱ, ㄷ, ㅂ, ㅅ, ㅈ’, the preceding consonant is pronounced
as an unreleased [ㅂ], and the following consonant is pronounced as a tense sound.
